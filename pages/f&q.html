F&Qs

What are the park's opening hours?
The park is open from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays and from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekends.
Is there parking available?
Yes, we have ample parking space available near the park entrance.
Can I bring outside food and drinks?
Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the park. We have various food stalls and restaurants offering delicious meals.
Are pets allowed in the park?
No, pets are not allowed inside the park for safety and hygiene reasons.
Do you offer discounts for groups?
Yes, we offer special discounts for groups with 20 or more people. Please contact our sales team for more information.
Are there ATMs available inside the park?
Yes, there are ATMs available near the park entrance and at various locations throughout the park.
Can I buy tickets online?
Yes, you can purchase tickets online through our website and mobile app.
Are there lockers available for personal belongings?
Yes, we provide lockers for visitors to store their personal belongings securely.
Do you have facilities for people with disabilities?
Yes, we have accessible facilities and services to accommodate visitors with disabilities.
Can I bring my own camera inside the park?
Yes, you can bring your own camera, but professional photography equipment and drones are not allowed.
